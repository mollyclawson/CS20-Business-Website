What We Do

If you want a radical change in your hairstyle without having to think about it, simply book an appointment with us.

Step One is the consultation, where we'll decide exactly what hairstyle will work best on you. We will asses your face shape, your fashion and makup style, and ask you questions about what celebrity's look you want to emulate. Every consultation is customized to the individual customer.

In Step Two, we'll get to working on your hair! Cut, color, perm, or blowout, we'll make sure that you walk out of Salon Metamorphosis feeling refreshed and renewed.

Who We Are

We are a staff of formally trained hair professionals. Among our staff are experts in hair color, curly hair care, perms, and so much more. We are haridressers to our core, but our #1 priority is always making sure that you leave our salon feeling confident.

Every member of our staff is trained to cut the hair of individuals of all genders and all races & ethnicities.
